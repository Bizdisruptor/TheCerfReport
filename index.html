What is The Cerf Report?

The Cerf Report is an independent publication covering the forces that actually shape the economy — trade policy, constitutional governance, geopolitics, and the technology systems rewriting the rules of power.

The goal is to follow the data, trace the incentives, and explain what daily coverage often leaves disconnected.

Every analysis stands on its own sources. No advertising. No agenda but accuracy.
